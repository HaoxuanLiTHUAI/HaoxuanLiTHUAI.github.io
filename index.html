About me

I am currently a PhD student in College of AI, Tsinghua University, supervised by Lu Mi. I am also a member from MAIC team, aiming at next-generation of AI empowered education, leading by Jifan Yu.

My research spans AI, Cognitive science, and AI for education, including human–AI cognitive alignment, agentic research for brain discovery, and human-centric AI systems for education. For a broader scope, I seek to understand the similarities and differences between human and artificial cognition and intelligence, examine how human cognition changes in the age of AI, and use these insights to guide human–AI cognitive collaboration, augmentation, and co-evolution toward human-centric AI.

I also lead BrainPilot, a fully open-source multi-agent platform for trustworthy brain science research, together with its evaluation benchmark, BrainPilotBench.
